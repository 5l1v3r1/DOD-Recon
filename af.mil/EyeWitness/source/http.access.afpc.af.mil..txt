This website was created to host publicly-accessible web applications for the general public as well as Air Force active duty, Guard, Reserve, and civilian members. For news and information, please visit the AFPC Public Website at <a href="http://www.afpc.af.mil" target="_blank">http://www.afpc.af.mil</a>. For total force personnel services information, visit <a href="http://mypers.af.mil" target="_blank">http://mypers.af.mil</a>.</p>
